# Isaac Sim

NVIDIA Isaac Sim is a robotics simulation platform built on top of NVIDIA Omniverse. It leverages the power of NVIDIA's RTX technology to create stunningly realistic and physically accurate virtual worlds for training and testing robots.

## Synthetic Data Generation

One of the key features of Isaac Sim is its ability to generate large-scale, high-quality synthetic datasets for training AI models.

**Replicator Composer**
- Powerful tool for creating synthetic data generation pipelines
- Procedurally generate diverse environments, objects, and scenarios
- Create comprehensive datasets automatically

**Domain Randomization**
- Randomly vary simulation parameters (lighting, friction, mass, textures)
- Improve robustness of AI models
- Expose models to wide range of conditions

## Realistic Simulation

Isaac Sim provides physically accurate simulation:

**PhysX 5.0**
- NVIDIA's physics engine for accurate rigid body, soft body, and fluid simulation
- Real-time performance
- Highly accurate contact and collision

**Sensor Models**
- Wide range of sensors: cameras, LiDAR, radar, IMUs
- Configurable to match real-world sensor specifications
- Realistic noise and characteristics

**Robot Models**
- Supports URDF and MJCF formats
- Growing library of pre-built robot models
- Easy to import custom robots
